## Speech

Investeasy enables investors and founders to immediately determine whether a startup idea is bankable and has real market potential. The platform identifies competitors within your niche, provides links to their landing pages, and assigns a feasibility and success score based on:

The type of idea

Market size

Niche and industry

Investeasy also analyzes competitor landing pages — and yours, if you wish — to help you understand your positioning relative to others in the market.

Investeasy is the investors’ favorite tool because it allows them to directly identify the most promising startups already operating in the market.

Investeasy vs ProductHunt: Investeasy wins — it compares real, existing products based on profitability and market traction.
Investeasy vs ChatGPT: Investeasy wins — it delivers direct, data-backed success scores without vague or generic feedback.
